About MAS Mahal

Our Story

MAS Mahal was established with a singular vision: to provide a luxurious yet affordable venue for the people of Tirupathur and surrounding areas. Located conveniently in Housing Board, we have become a landmark for auspicious beginnings.

We pride ourselves on offering a "Divine Environment" where families can come together to celebrate love, joy, and milestones. Our management and staff are dedicated to ensuring that every event hosted with us is flawless, hygienic, and memorable.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional hospitality and a safe, comfortable venue for all our guests. We prioritize cleanliness, guest safety, and efficient service above all else.
